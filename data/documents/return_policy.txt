Return Policy

Customers may request a return within 14 days of receiving their order. To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase.

Refunds are processed after the returned item is inspected by our support team. Once approved, refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.

Certain items are not eligible for returns, including personalized products, gift cards, and downloadable software. Products damaged due to misuse or normal wear and tear are also excluded.

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was delivered damaged or incorrect. In such cases, the company will provide a prepaid return label.

For assistance with returns, customers may contact our support team through email or live chat. Our support team is available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM.
