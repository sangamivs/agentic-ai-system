Shipping Information

Orders are processed within 2 business days after payment confirmation. Standard shipping typically takes 3 to 5 business days depending on the destination.

Customers receive a tracking number by email once the order has been shipped. Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to become active.

Express shipping options are available during checkout for selected locations. Delivery times for express shipping usually range from 1 to 2 business days.

International shipping is available to selected countries. Delivery times typically range from 7 to 14 business days.

If an order has not arrived within the expected delivery window, customers should contact support for assistance. Lost shipments are investigated by the logistics team and updates are provided within 48 hours.
